## Residue Ladder Patch

Sharpen the distinction:

```text
canonical residue = survives absorption and appears forced, natural, or
unavoidable across the declared observer/task/horizon/resource boundary.
```

```text
formal residue = survives absorption as a typed distinction, but canonicity is
not established.
```

```text
translation residue = no new formal phenomenon, but the audit surface aligns
mature theories usefully.
```

```text
heuristic residue = helps find missing state, access, provenance, resource, or
policy data.
```

```text
redundant/demoted = adds confusion or duplicates prior art without useful
review value.
```

Editorial rule:

```text
Default expectation remains translation or heuristic residue.
Canonical residue requires proof-level discipline.
```
