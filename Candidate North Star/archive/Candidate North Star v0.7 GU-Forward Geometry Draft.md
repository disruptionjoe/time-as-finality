## What Is Established Geometry

The following claims are not GU-specific. They are part of the established
geometric language of modern physics.

### Gravity

In general relativity, spacetime is modeled by a 4-dimensional manifold `X`
equipped with a Lorentzian metric `g`. The metric determines causal structure,
length, time, light cones, and the Levi-Civita connection. Curvature encodes
gravity.

In this sense:

```text
gravity is geometry of metric, connection, curvature, and causal access
```

This does not mean all physics has been derived from geometry. It means the
gravitational part of known physics already forces us to treat observer access,
causal reachability, horizons, domains of dependence, and finite-time
operations geometrically.

### Gauge Fields

In classical gauge theory, a gauge field is geometrically represented by a
connection on a principal bundle. Its field strength is curvature.

For the Standard Model skeleton, the internal gauge symmetry is usually
organized around:

```text
SU(3) x SU(2) x U(1)
```

The local fields physicists call gluon, weak, and electromagnetic gauge fields
are not arbitrary labels. In the classical geometric skeleton, they are local
connection data and curvature data for the relevant internal symmetry bundles.

Quantum particles are not literally identical to a classical connection.
Particles are excitations after quantization. But the geometric substrate of
the field content is already bundle-and-connection geometry.

### Fermions And Spin

Fermions are where the geometry becomes most revealing and easiest to
overstate.

The honest established statement is:

```text
Spinor fields are sections of spinor bundles.
```

A spinor bundle exists when the manifold has the required spin structure, or
the relevant generalized spin/spin-c structure. The spinor bundle is associated
to a principal Spin bundle through a spin representation. The Dirac operator is
built from the spin connection and Clifford multiplication.

So spin does not come from an ordinary tensor. Spinors are not ordinary tensors
for the rotation/Lorentz group. They come from the Spin double cover and the
Clifford algebra representation theory attached to the tangent geometry.

In the Standard Model, fermion fields are spinor fields additionally carrying
internal gauge representations. Geometrically, this means:

```text
fermion field ~= section of spinor bundle tensor associated internal bundles
```

where the internal bundles are associated to representations of
`SU(3) x SU(2) x U(1)`.

What this explains:

- why spinor geometry is the right home for fermion fields;
- why chirality is naturally expressed through the splitting of spinor
  representations in even dimension;
- why internal quantum numbers can be represented by associated bundles;
- why a connection is needed to differentiate matter fields covariantly.

What this does not explain by itself:

- why the observed fermion masses have their values;
- why the Yukawa matrices have their exact entries;
- why there are exactly three observed generations;
- why quantization produces the full measured Standard Model;
- why GU, specifically, is correct.

### Higgs And Scalars

The Higgs field is also naturally represented geometrically as a section of an
associated vector bundle. In physics language it is a scalar field with
internal gauge representation. In geometric language it is not merely "a
number everywhere"; it is a section whose transformation behavior is fixed by
the associated bundle and representation.

### Holonomy And Phase

Connections produce parallel transport. Transport around a loop can return
with nontrivial holonomy. This is not metaphor. Holonomy is a geometric object.

The local observer may see compatible patches while the loop as a whole carries
an obstruction. That is why Cech cocycles, monodromy, Berry phases, Wilson
loops, and gauge holonomy are so naturally adjacent to Candidate North Star:

```text
local shadow agreement does not always determine global capability
```

Again, the phenomenon is old. The proposed value is that Capability Projection
keeps the observer, projection, comparison, and residue bookkeeping explicit.


```text

```

This is the cleanest GU-facing picture for Candidate North Star:
