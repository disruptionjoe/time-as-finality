## What could still survive as genuine residue

A genuine surviving contribution would have to move away from the bare slogan and toward a **rigorous comparison theorem across fields**. One plausible surviving project would define a task-indexed capability object and then prove systematic translations to: Blackwell sufficiency for static decisions; belief-state sufficiency for partial observation; testing/bisimulation-style equivalences for interactive systems; resource convertibility preorders for admissible operations; and causal/interventional distinctions for intervention queries.
