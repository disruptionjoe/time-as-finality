## The joint effort (stated with guards intact)

The three repositories independently drove three different questions to the
same structural residue:

> A bounded system, complete and self-consistent in itself, provably cannot
> supply one specific thing from inside — and the missing thing lives at its
> boundary. The honest ledger for that boundary is what the region can do,
> not what it can see.

- **time-as-finality:** whether a record is read or ignored moves zero process
  statistics (T395); capability profiles admit no scalar ranking and are not
  determined by declared statistics (T397, finite-witness, partially absorbed
  by T398 at the resource-preorder level); the arrow reappears as capability
  loss across an access boundary.
- **gu-formalization:** the interior matter geometry is vectorlike and cannot
  force its own chirality / generation count; the count enters as external
  boundary data.
- **temporal-issuance:** an interior cannot precontain its own genuine
  extension; if novelty enters, it enters across the interface.

**The identity claim ("one object, three shadows") is explicitly NOT made.**
Per `audits/2026-07-01-tri-repo-synthesis-hegelian-metasynthesis.md`, only an
interface-contract signature survives, gated behind adapter theorems: no
identity claim before at least two adapters prove out. Current status: same
shape, three independent arrivals, identity untested.
