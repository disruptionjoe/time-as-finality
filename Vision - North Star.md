# Candidate North Star v0.8: Maximal Geometry Vision

## Status

This is a speculative North Star draft.

It is intentionally not the conservative research posture from v0.6.
It is intentionally not a proof-limited document.
It is a vision document for what we think might be true if the geometry is
real.

The boundary is:

```text
Do not assert what established mathematics or physics already proves wrong.
```

The boundary is not:

```text
Only say what established physics has already proved.
```

That second boundary is too small for a North Star. It traps the project in
incremental local minima. The rest of the repo can play defense. This document
is allowed to point at the mountain.

## Core Claim

We believe the world available to human observers is a shadow of a deeper
geometric structure.

We believe humans are not outside the system looking in. We are physical,
evolved observer-processes inside the system. Our bodies, instruments, nervous
systems, memories, records, and conscious access are all ways the system
samples itself locally.

We believe the stable things physics names as particles, forces, spin, charge,
measurement, causal access, thermodynamic direction, record finality, and
observer capability are not separate mysteries floating above geometry. They
are what geometry looks like under internal observation.

The target is not a branded theory. The target is the underlying mathematics:

```text
4-dimensional observed interaction base
+ higher geometric/capability structure
+ observer projection
+ loss or preservation of capability under that projection
```

If the geometric shape works, it works. The job of the repo is then to prove it
or break it.

## The Research Boundary

v0.8 does not ask:

```text
Has this already been proved by established physics?
```

It asks:

```text
Is this shape already ruled out?
```

If a claim is ruled out by established mathematics or physics, it must be cut.
If a claim is not ruled out but remains unproved, it may stay as a target,
provided it is stated as a target and has a path toward typing, testing,
demotion, or proof.

This gives the project a different posture:

- The North Star may be aggressive.
- The proof program must be merciless.
- The repo exists to kill the vision if the vision is false.
- Surviving structure is allowed to become stronger.

inside that North Star.

The North Star itself is the geometry.

The higher structure `E` should not be read automatically as extra physical
spacetime dimensions. It is a total space of geometric, capability, field,
record, boundary, and access data over the observed base. A future model may
earn a physical-dimensional interpretation, but v0.8 does not need that claim.

The bet is that these are not merely analogies. They are fragments of one

## The Observer-Shadow Thesis

The full state is not what an internal observer receives.

An observer receives a shadow:

```text
pi_O(y)
```

where `y` is the richer source geometry and `O` is an embedded observer/access
profile.

For humans, `O` includes body, nervous system, instruments, memory, records,
language, attention, action limits, causal location, energy limits, and the
evolved tasks that shaped our perception.

Consciousness is not introduced here as a magic extra substance. In this draft,
consciousness names the lived access mode of an internal observer-process. It
is part of the access profile unless and until a deeper theory types it more
fundamentally.

The central question is:

```text
What does the shadow preserve?
```

Not:

```text
What is the shadow made of phenomenologically?
```

but:

```text
For a task family T, horizon h, and boundary B, does pi_O(y) determine
Cap_{O,T,h,B}(y)?
```

This is the bridge between the vision and the existing audit language.

## Fermions, Spin, And Matter

The strongest safe-aggressive statement:

```text
Fermions are not arbitrary particles sprinkled onto spacetime. In the
geometric skeleton of physics, fermion fields are spinor-bundle sections,
twisted by internal representation data.
```

Spin is not an ordinary tensor property. Spinors come from Spin geometry,
Clifford modules, and the double-cover relationship between Spin groups and
rotation/Lorentz groups. On a spin manifold, the spinor bundle is associated
to a principal Spin bundle through a spin representation. The Dirac operator
uses the spin connection and Clifford multiplication.

The Standard Model's local/classical skeleton adds internal representation
structure. In geometric language:

```text
fermion field =
section of spinor bundle tensor associated internal representation bundles
```

This does not prove the particle spectrum. It does not determine masses,
mixing angles, Yukawa couplings, or generation count.

It also does not settle global-form subtleties of the Standard Model gauge
group, finite quotients, anomaly cancellation, or representation constraints.
Those details matter and belong in the proof program.

But it strongly suggests that matter is not external to geometry. Matter is
geometry seen through representation, spin, section, and quantization.

The vision:

```text
what appears to us as spin + internal quantum number + chirality may be the
observer-shadow split of a more unified geometric object.
```

That may be wrong. It is not obviously ruled out by the fact that established
physics already describes fermions through spinor and associated bundles. The
burden is to find the exact object or prove no such object exists.

## Forces And Gauge Geometry

The strongest safe-aggressive statement:

```text
Forces are not merely pushes. In the geometric skeleton, gauge interactions
are connection and curvature phenomena.
```

A connection tells fields how to compare values across nearby points. Its
curvature records failure of transport to close trivially. Gauge symmetry
controls which descriptions count as the same physical state.

The local/classical Standard Model symmetry is usually organized around:

```text
SU(3) x SU(2) x U(1)
```

is already telling us that physics is representation-constrained geometry.
The exact global form can involve finite quotients and representation/anomaly
constraints, so this line should be read as the familiar local gauge-algebra
and local-bundle skeleton, **not as a completed global classification**.

The vision is not that every force has already been derived. The vision is
that every force we understand deeply appears to become more intelligible when
read as structure in a bundle-like geometry:

```text
connection -> transport
curvature -> field strength
holonomy -> global memory of local transport
representation -> charge and coupling channel
section -> matter or order parameter
```

## Dark Matter And Dark Energy As Stress Tests

v0.8 does not say:

```text
dark matter is projection loss
dark energy is capability loss
```

Those are too sloppy.

The bolder but typed claim is:

```text
dark matter and dark energy are stress tests for whether visible shadows are
missing geometric capability data.
```

For dark matter, the question is whether the luminous/electromagnetic shadow
is insufficient for gravitational dynamics under a declared model.

For dark energy, the question is whether cosmological geometry changes future
causal access: what regions, records, signals, and comparisons remain
available to internal observers.

This may collapse entirely into established cosmology. If so, good. The
absorber won.

But the North Star is allowed to ask the larger question:

```text
Are the missing pieces telling us about unseen stuff inside spacetime, or about
the insufficiency of the observer-shadow geometry we are using?
```

That question is allowed as a research target.

## What Would Prove This Wrong

This vision should be killable.

It weakens if:

- the needed higher geometry cannot be typed without contradictions;
- the proposed observer-shadow cannot be defined as a real projection or
  access map;
- spin, gauge, causal, record, and capability structures refuse to live in a
  shared mathematical setting;
- every apparent residue is absorbed by mature theory with no useful transfer;
- the finite TaF witnesses cannot be bridged to smooth or physical geometry;
- the capability objects turn out to be gerrymandered;
- gauge-dependent distinctions are mistaken for physical distinctions;
- the vision makes no predictions, no minimality theorem, no executable
  witnesses, and no demotion rules.

It is not weakened merely because it is unproved.

Unproved is the point.

## What Is Not Allowed

These would be too strong or wrong:

- Spinors are ordinary tensors.
- Every manifold automatically carries the spin or spin-c structure needed for
  fermions.
- Gauge-related descriptions are different physical states just because their
  coordinates differ.
- A 4-dimensional local shadow determines the full global state.
- Classical bundle geometry by itself derives quantum probabilities.
- A finite combinatorial witness automatically proves a smooth field theorem.
- A geometric metaphor solves dark matter or dark energy.
- Consciousness is inserted as an untyped physical force.
- Capability is a single scalar that replaces all physics.
- The Standard Model spectrum, masses, mixings, and couplings have already
  been recovered by this framework.

These are not the vision. These are failure modes.

## The Proof Program

The repo should now be read as a proof-against engine for v0.8.

The required work:

1. Type the source geometry.
2. Type the observer-shadow projection.
3. Type capability objects for concrete domains.
4. Prove gauge invariance or reject the witness.
5. Define causal/access structure before using horizons or reachability.
6. Bridge finite TaF witnesses to smooth geometry only through declared
   functors, discretizations, or limit theorems.
7. Run native absorber tests before promoting residue.
8. Search for minimal enrichments that restore shadow protection.
9. Search for one cross-domain theorem that survives GR, gauge theory,
   quantum contextuality, detector provenance, and record finality.
10. Let failed witnesses die.

This is how a vision avoids becoming a religion.
