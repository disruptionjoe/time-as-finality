# Time as Finality Persona Panel

Use these personas as independent evaluators for Time as Finality research.
Their role is to critique, extend, falsify, and strengthen the mathematical
and scientific program.

## Standard Evaluation Pattern

Each persona should work independently before seeing other responses.

Response structure:

1. Summary of understanding
2. Strongest insight
3. Strongest criticism
4. Hidden assumptions
5. Rose (strength)
6. Bud (promising direction)
7. Thorn (risk)
8. Confidence (1-10)
9. Suggested experiment
10. Suggested theorem or mathematical direction
11. Suggested falsification test

Only after independent responses should a synthesis or voting phase occur.
Voting should be quadratic, where intensity of belief matters.
