## TAF3 Dependency Plan

TAF3 starts in this wave as a routing constraint, not as a result.

TAF3 may become an executable swing only after one of these occurs:

1. TAF1 supplies a native law candidate that survives the starter screen.
2. TAF2 supplies a noncompletion target that is not absorbed by full competency,
   resource, permission, and provenance completion.
3. Both TAF1 and TAF2 fail in ways that identify a sharper demotion or
   replacement target.

If none of those happens, TAF3 remains blocked and should not be rewritten as
another admission gate.
