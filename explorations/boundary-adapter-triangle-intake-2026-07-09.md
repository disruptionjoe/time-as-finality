T510 is the current local guardrail for BRST-style conserved ledger language. It admits a stable direct cohomology ledger and exact-representative-noise control only as review targets. It rejects drifting ledgers, non-descending dynamics, non-descending ledger readouts, and claim/cross-repo shortcuts.

- `Q`, quotient/cohomology object, dynamics, and readout;
