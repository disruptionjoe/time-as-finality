## Result 1: H1 as the measure of co-creation failure (new reading of the existing obstruction)

The sheaf geometer and the Bach/counterpoint lens independently converged (and the decoherence and
formal-methods lenses concurred): **"shared reality" is a gluing/descent question, and local finality does NOT
entail a global shared world.** Each observer's finalized binding is a *local section* of the finality sheaf;
shared reality is a *global section*; and a global section exists only when the Cech class
**H1(U, F_finality)** vanishes. When H1 != 0, observers co-create worlds that are **locally coherent and
pairwise-agreeing yet globally incompatible** -- there is no single shared reality, only a cocycle of
mutually-consistent fragments.

This **reinterprets the existing T13/T16 H1 obstruction**: it is not only a measurement-problem no-go, it is
the precise **measure of when co-creating observers fail to share one world**. It also vindicates the repo's
no-global-now posture: a single shared "now"/world is the *exceptional* (H1 = 0) case, not the default.

Bounded lane: extend T13's canonical cover so each domain is an *observer's finalized-binding patch*, and read
H1 as co-creation-failure. This is the same machinery as the free-boundary sprint's "coboundary-repair" lane
(is the obstruction removable?) -- coordinate with it. Local hardening (deep stalks) is necessary but not
sufficient for a global section; the ratchet/reversal-cost machinery lives in the stalks and is *blind* to H1.

## Result 2 (PROPOSAL, needs Joe's sign-off): a positive companion to G1/G3

Joe's question was whether observer-level issuance *violates* the guardrails G1 (human belief does not create
matter) and G3 (observer rendering is not mind-created reality). The council's unanimous finding: **it is a
reframe, not a violation -- iff one verb changes.** Replace "create" with **bind/select within a fixed
possibility-space**. Then observers author neither matter nor substrate; they differ only in which
already-possible transformations are available to them, and finality is conferred *externally*.

Proposed companion principle (to sit *beside* G1/G3, not replace them), for Joe to accept/revise/reject:

> **G4 (proposed): Observers bind candidates; finality decides what becomes real for everyone.**
> Observers are genuine local *sources of bindings* (issuance), selecting within a fixed possibility-space they
> do not author. A binding becomes a shared fact only through *external* finality -- rising reversal cost
> across distinct holders, never the observer's own attestation -- and becomes a *globally* shared fact only
> where the gluing obstruction (H1) vanishes. This neither weakens G1/G3 nor admits mind-created matter: it
> states the positive content G1/G3 leave implicit.
