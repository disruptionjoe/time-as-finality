## Purpose (prep, not a swing)

Set the foundation for **M2** (meta-synthesis next-move #2): move the recurring
"global section with no local witness" object (finality = legitimacy = the
capability separator) from **rhyme to computed** by exhibiting a *concrete*

## The bar M2 must clear (inherited from T422)

T422 delivered the span at **finite-witness synthesis tier** but its core was
**category-relative** — there was *no single canonical category* in which E1/E3
are representable and E2 is not. **M2's whole job is to beat that: exhibit a
CANONICAL object** (an operator or a game) whose index/value *is* the finality
datum independent of a hand-picked category. Canonicity is the success bar; a
category-relative object is a REDESIGN.

**The canonicity strategy this scout surfaced:** the same "global-no-local" object
appears — independently — as three established formalizations:
1. the **APS boundary eta-invariant** of a Dirac operator on a manifold *with
   boundary* (index = interior integral + a boundary term that is a genuine
   global-no-local datum);
2. the **Aumann–Shapley non-atomic value** (a global datum carried by no finite
   coalition — TaF's own T413 lineage);
3. the **judgment-aggregation collective judgment** (List–Pettit): a group verdict
   that provably cannot be reconstructed from local individual judgments (the
   discursive dilemma) — the social-epistemology form of "no local witness."
**If a single M2 object is simultaneously an APS-eta term, an Aumann–Shapley value,
AND a judgment-aggregation obstruction, that triple-coincidence is the canonicity
T422 lacked** — the object is not category-relative because three independent
categories compute the same thing.
