# Cluster E — Philosophy / Testability / Biology / Cognitive

The cluster question, stated flat: **finality, physical-boundary, and legitimacy
keep appearing as the "global thing with no local witness." Is that one object
under four names (unification) or four objects sharing a suggestive word
(equivocation)?** The rigorous-heterodox job is to separate those two verdicts and
not let poetry launder the second into the first.

## 19 — Causal Inference (Marcus Hale)

**Meta-synthesis.** The whole four-mode taxonomy is a **theory of when SEE
underdetermines DO** — when the observational algebra (E0: datum co-present, just
un-queried) fails to fix the interventional answer, and *what resource forces the
gap*: sampling-to-the-limit (E1), computational reach (E2), or an invariance/no-go
(E3). "Physical vs declared" is exactly Pearl's observational-vs-interventional
rung, and the forcing trichotomy names the three ways a do-operator becomes
non-identifiable from passive data. The profound object is not finality — it's a
**general classification of identifiability failure by the resource that causes
it.**

**Hegelian.** Antithesis: identifiability theory already has a back-door/adjustment
calculus; "you renamed non-identifiability." True for E0/E1. But the calculus does
*not* sort failures by *why* — energy vs hardness vs symmetry — and treats
computational cost as free (all identifiable functionals are equally "computed").
E2 (identifiable-in-principle, unreachable-in-practice) is a genuinely
under-theorized rung. Synthesis: survives as a **resource-graded refinement of the
do-calculus**, novel at E2, derivative at E0/E1.

**Steelman.** Concrete claim: define a boundary as *interventionally forced* iff no
finite-sample, poly-time, symmetry-respecting estimator recovers the separating
datum, and prove the three forcing modes are **mutually irreducible** (a gap of
one type cannot be converted to another). The one move that makes it real: a
**convertibility no-go between E1, E2, E3** — the same theorem shape the cluster
keeps circling. Without it, it's taxonomy; with it, it's structure.

## 28 — Evolutionary Biologist

**Meta-synthesis.** Observer-boundedness is **adaptive, not accidental.** An agent
that could query every datum in the global algebra would carry unbounded record
cost; selection favors organisms that *forget* — that let boundaries stay
"declared" (cheap) until a resource forces them physical. Finality (irreversible
commitment of a record) is the evolutionary analogue of **canalization /
irreversible fixation**: a lineage that has committed cannot cheaply un-commit.
The arrow is what a replicator experiences because reversing a fixed record costs
more than the fitness gain of reversing it.

**Hegelian.** Antithesis: this is metaphor — "fixation" and "finality" rhyme but
one is population-genetic, the other informational; you've imported the arrow you
wanted to explain (selection already presupposes time). Fair: evolution can't
*ground* the arrow without circularity. Synthesis: survives not as a grounding but
as an **instantiation** — living systems are a worked example of a boundary made
physical by a *maintenance cost*, which is a real, measurable quantity, not a
rhyme.

**Steelman.** Concrete claim: a boundary is E1-forced *for an agent* iff the energy
to recover the separating datum exceeds the agent's dissipation budget over its
commitment horizon. That's a **fitness-relative, thermodynamic** definition of
"physical boundary" — testable in principle (measure the recovery cost vs the
metabolic budget). The one move: show finality's forcing mode is E1 (metabolic)
for living observers, distinct from E2/E3 forcing elsewhere — which would make the
arrow *substrate-relative*, not universal.

## 29 — Systems Biologist

**Meta-synthesis.** The "global section with no local witness" (item 10, the
H^1 residue) is the everyday structure of **homeostasis / regulatory closure**: no
single molecule carries "the cell is alive"; it is a global property of the
network's maintained non-equilibrium. Legitimacy, finality, and
capability-boundedness are all this same **dissipation-maintained global
invariant** — a property that exists only while the system spends energy to hold
it, and vanishes locally the moment you cut any subset out. The missing assembly:
these are not four analogies to a cohomology class, they are four instances of a
*maintained* global section, and maintenance (energy throughput) is the
common substrate.

**Hegelian.** Antithesis: "maintained global invariant" is so general it fits
everything — you've found a category, not a mechanism; and cohomology obstructions
are *static* (no energy term), so bolting "dissipation" on is category error.
Real bite. Synthesis: survives only if you distinguish **static gluing
obstruction (H^1, no cost)** from **actively-maintained global property (costs
power)** — and the program's own T421 disanalogy (operational-forced vs
metric-forced) is exactly this split surfacing. So 29's contribution is to *name
the split*, not erase it.

**Steelman.** Concrete claim: partition boundaries into **static-forced** (symmetry/
cohomology, E3-metric, zero maintenance) vs **maintenance-forced** (thermodynamic,
E1, positive power draw), and predict finality lives in the second class. The one
move: a maintenance monotone — a quantity that is zero for declared boundaries,
positive for maintenance-forced ones, and *additive under composition of
subsystems* — i.e. exactly the sublinear/non-additive mu-candidate the metabolic
persona keeps asking for.

## 30 — Neuroscientist

**Meta-synthesis.** The unifying object is **the observer as record-renderer.** A
brain does not perceive the world; it commits an irreversible record (memory
trace, synaptic change) and *that commitment is what "now" is*. Finality =
the write becoming un-rewritable; the arrow = the direction in which the predictive
model's records accumulate. The four names collapse because all four describe the
*same act from different sides*: physical-boundary (what the observer cannot
recover), finality (what it has committed), legitimacy (what the whole system
ratifies as committed), capability (what it can still do). One process:
**recording under a resource bound.**

**Hegelian.** Antithesis: "observer-relative recording" is interpretation-flavored
hand-waving — you've swapped a physics question for a neuroscience metaphor and
smuggled consciousness in. And predictive-processing already owns this; nothing
new. Synthesis: survives *only* stripped of phenomenology, as an **information-
theoretic claim about irreversible writes under a dissipation bound** — Landauer,
not qualia. Then it's not new neuroscience; it's a demand that the *observer* be a
formal object in the taxonomy, which the taxonomy currently leaves implicit.

**Steelman.** Concrete claim: the taxonomy is *incomplete without an explicit
observer parameter* — the admissible-menu/observable-algebra (item 7) IS the
observer, and E0-vs-forced is **relative to it**. Make the observer a first-class
index and "physical vs declared" becomes a two-place relation, not a property. The
one move: show that fixing the observable algebra (the observer) is what selects
E0 vs E3 — which item 7 already asserts and item 8 shows can be conflated.

## 40 — Legal Scholar

**Meta-synthesis.** Read the whole thing as **jurisdiction.** "Physical vs declared"
is *forced-by-the-world* vs *declared-by-a-authority*; the Aumann-Shapley
legitimacy ladder (item 9) and the ratification gate (item 11) are the mechanism
that turns a declared boundary into a binding one. Legitimacy = a global property
with no local representative is *precisely* how law treats sovereignty and
precedent: no single citizen or ruling carries "this is binding"; it inheres in
the whole system's ratification. Finality is **res judicata** — the point past
which a record cannot be re-litigated. The program has, unknowingly, a
**constructor theory of when a commitment becomes non-appealable.**

**Hegelian.** Antithesis: law is a normative human institution; importing it to
physics is the reverse of the usual overreach and equally illicit — "binding" is
a social fact, "forced" is a physical one, and collapsing them is the exact
equivocation this cluster is hunting. Synthesis: survives as a **structural
homology of ratification**, not an identity. Both domains share: *a global gate
that converts revisable → final, with no local witness to the conversion.* That
gate-shape is real and repeated; the physics-vs-declared distinction is preserved,
not erased — declared boundaries need ratification, forced ones don't.

**Steelman.** Concrete claim: finality is the fixed point of a **ratification
operator** — a boundary is final iff every admissible future observer (the whole
menu, item 7) would ratify the record; this is a global section over the observer
class with no local section. The one move: identify the ratification operator with
the Aumann-Shapley value (the unique axiom-forced global assignment) and show
res-judicata = the non-atomic limit. That makes "legitimacy at the infinite
layers" a *definition of finality*, not a metaphor for it.

## 44 — Ecologist

**Meta-synthesis.** Finality is a **panarchy phenomenon**: the irreversible release

**Hegelian.** Antithesis: panarchy is itself a loose metaphor-heavy framework;

weakness — living/ecological finality may be **uniformly thermodynamic**, which
would mean the biological cluster (28/29/44) collectively argues the *arrow is
E1*, distinct from GU's E3. The disanalogy T421 predicted exactly this split.

**Steelman.** Concrete claim: for any dissipative nested system, finality = crossing
a hysteresis threshold whose recross-energy exceeds the mobilizable-power budget
within a reorganization time — a fully E1, resource-relative, measurable
definition. The one move: unify 28/29/44 into a single **"living finality is
maintenance-forced (E1)"** thesis and contrast it against non-living
metric-forced (E3) finality, making forcing-mode the real taxonomy axis.

## 53 — North Star Visionary

**Meta-synthesis.** The single most profound rigorous-heterodox story: **Time is
the name we give to the fact that some boundaries must be paid for.** A declared
boundary is free and reversible (no arrow); a *forced* boundary costs a resource to
maintain or to cross, and the direction of increasing paid-for-commitment **is**
the arrow. Finality, legitimacy, capability-boundedness, and the physical/declared
line are one thing seen from four sides: **the ledger of what has been paid for and
cannot be refunded.** The North Star is not "time is thermodynamic" — it's the

forcing resources.*"

**Hegelian.** Antithesis: this is the most seductive and therefore most dangerous
statement in the whole cluster — it's the metaphor 42 warned about, dressed as
vision, and it risks *contaminating* the earned mathematics by making everything
"about time." Protect the math from it. Synthesis: keep it as **North Star, not
theorem** — reached-for, quarantined. Its legitimate function is to *orient* the
convertibility theorem (51) and the translation test (41), not to license claims.
It survives precisely as long as it is not cashed prematurely.

**Steelman.** Concrete claim, held at arm's length: if forcing is a resource theory
(51) and the resources are non-interconvertible, then "irreversible expenditure"
is a **well-defined monotone** and the arrow is its increase — making time a
*derived quantity of the forcing resource theory*, not a primitive. The one move:
the convertibility theorem. If it proves a basis, the North Star becomes reachable
mathematics; if it proves a lattice-with-top, the vision collapses to "it's all
E1" (28/29/44 win). Either way the *theorem*, not the vision, decides.
