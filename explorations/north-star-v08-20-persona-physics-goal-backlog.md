# North Star v0.8 20-Persona Physics Goal Backlog

## Purpose

This panel treats the v0.8 maximal geometry vision as real North Star material
while keeping Time as Finality and Capability Projection as the active proof
discipline.

The goal is not to promote physics claims. The goal is to generate bounded
research goals that can clarify, support, weaken, or kill the physics-facing
parts of the vision by testing the mathematics.

## Routing Rule

These goals are exploratory backlog items, not claim updates, roadmap
commitments, or evidence. A goal may affect claim status only after it produces
a durable artifact that passes the ordinary repo gates:

- typed source geometry or known theory;
- declared observer-shadow projection;
- declared capability object and native comparison;
- gauge, coordinate, relabeling, and absorber checks where relevant;
- positive controls and hostile controls;
- explicit demotion or kill condition.

The main line remains:

```text
Time as Finality + Capability Projection
  -> typed witnesses
  -> absorber passes
  -> demotion or promotion
```

The North Star lane is allowed to ask bigger geometry questions, but it must
pay rent through this audit path.

## Synthesis Verdict

The panel found five convergence signals.

1. The highest-value North Star work is not another physics analogy. It is a
   typed geometry-to-capability audit template that can be run against quantum
   measurement, thermodynamic arrow, causal access, spin/gauge structure, and
   horizons.
2. The most dangerous failure mode is gauge or coordinate confusion. Any
   physics-facing shadow distinction that changes under gauge, relabeling, or
   coordinate convention should be rejected or downgraded to an access-boundary
   statement.
3. The finite-to-smooth bridge is now a central blocker. Finite TaF witnesses
   can keep guiding the search, but v0.8 needs declared functors, limits, or
   discretization maps before smooth geometry language can carry theorem
   weight.
4. The most ambitious live idea is a cross-domain shadow-protection theorem:
   an observer shadow is capability-sufficient exactly when it preserves the
   minimal geometric data needed for transport, access, representation,
   boundary, record, and operation structure.
5. The fastest way to make the North Star scientific is a kill matrix for each
   physics sentence in v0.8: what would absorb it, what would falsify it, and
   what artifact would earn a stronger version.

## Highest-Value Backlog Goals

### NS-P01 - Physics Claim Inventory And Known-Theory Cap Audit

- persona: Mathematical Physicist
- route: mathematical machinery; all physics routes
- status: queued
- goal: Rewrite every physics-facing v0.8 claim in the No-Free-Physics
  template: known theory, source state, observer/access profile, projection,
  induced capability object, native comparison, absorber, residue status, and
  not-claimed boundary.
- first artifact: `explorations/north-star-v08-physics-claim-inventory.md`.
- why worthy: This is the shortest path from vision to falsifiability. It
  tells the repo which physics sentences are already absorbed, which are only
  geometry-language examples, and which can become real test targets.
- support condition: at least one physics domain yields a typed capability
  audit whose residue is not immediately redundant.
- kill condition: if a claim cannot derive `Cap` from known physics, it remains
  vision-only and should not enter paper-facing language.

## Non-Promotion Rule

No item in this panel upgrades a physics claim. The intended outputs are
research notes, open problems, test specs, guardrails, and demotion artifacts.

The North Star becomes stronger only if its physics sentences survive these
goals. If they fail, the project becomes stronger by knowing exactly where the
vision breaks.
