## Phase 3 — Metasynthesis (convergence clusters)

Six convergence clusters formed across the independent Hegelian passes. For each:
what it is, who landed on it, and — held to the single-process ceiling — what it
does and does not establish.

### M1. The finite-closed-model ceiling (the strongest convergence)

**Claim.** In a finite **closed unitary** model, a strict "physically forced"
capability boundary is generically **ill-posed**: unitarity keeps everything
co-present and recoverable, so every declared cut is definable-from-inside, hence
declared. The kill history is not a run of bad luck; it is this theorem showing
through three times.

**Independent arrivals (all 8 clusters; ~40 personas), each a different mask:**
- **Definability / automorphism-invariance** (A: 26,15,5) — finite closed ⇒
  definable ⇒ declared; "physical" = fixed by the model's automorphism group /
  not definable from the agent's relations.
- **Cover-dependence of cohomology** (E: 4,45,3,48) — the surviving residue is a
  genuine Čech H¹ non-gluing class, but H¹ is cover-relative and joint-record
  completion **is** cover refinement, which sends H¹→0.
- **Forgeability / retention** (C: 36; F: 57,60,61) — physical iff the crossing
  credential is unforgeable from R **and** the pre-image is retained nowhere;
  `partial trace ≠ deletion`; shift the certificate from "R cannot **see** the
  pre-image" to "the full machine does not **retain** it" (globally many-to-one,
  not R-local exclusion).
- **Arrow-not-boundary** (D: 22,31,32) — DPI / info-bottleneck / controllability
  each force a *direction/monotone*, but leave *where R ends* declared.
- **Limit-property** (B: all five) — physical forcing is a property of a
  limit/sequence, not of any finite closed model.
- **Typing-not-forcing** (H: 19) — causal structure *types* the boundary
  (observational ≠ interventional is the right axis) but cannot *force* it in a
  closed model, where the do-cut is menu-relative and menu-relativity is the
  declaration wound.
- **Budget-relative reachability** (G: all eight) — "physical" is well-posed only
  as dynamics-indexed, agent-relative, budget-relative reachability; not
  description-independent.

**Establishes:** a high-value **target** — a *scope theorem*: prove that in
finite closed models the boundary is provably a definability/automorphism
artifact, so a non-declarable boundary requires either infinite structure or an
asymptotic (complexity/thermodynamic) gap. Several personas (A: 15,26,14; G: 27)
argue this negative is the most valuable output of the whole program.
**Does not establish:** that no clever finite closed fixture exists — only that
six independent disciplines expect none, which is a prior, not a proof.

### M2. Dynamically-derived irreversibility is the *only* escape

**Claim.** The single thing that defeats **both** joint-record completion **and**
the wait-longer killer is genuine, dynamically-**derived** irreversibility —
departure that leaves the pre-image co-present *nowhere* — as opposed to unitary
spread plus a **stipulated** partial trace.

**Independent arrivals (B,C,D,E,F,H,A), one condition in many dialects:**
move-not-copy exact linearity (A: 37); globally many-to-one update (F: 57,59,17);
forced decoupling / proxy-decay (D: 32,22,12; H: 20,29); budget-irreducible
curvature / holonomy over the coupling graph (E: 48); dissipation-maintained
non-equilibrium steady state whose departed record is maintenance-unreachable
(H: 29,63); entropy-priced permanence (B; C-56 dissent; H).

**The admission price (explicit in B,E,F,H).** This forcing lives *outside* the
finite closed regime. It must be **derived** in a controlled limit (open model,
large-N, bounded error) — because if the irreversibility is simply *adopted*
(a hand-imposed partial trace, a stipulated reservoir), it re-imports the exact
reservoir-idealization absorber that fired `boundary_physicality_reduces_to_
declaration` on T410/T411 ("adopted, not derived").

**Establishes:** the design invariant every redesign must satisfy.
**Does not establish:** that any in-house finite-witness model can host derived
(vs adopted) dissipation — this is precisely the unpaid price.

### M3. The global-correlation separator is the surviving *object* (an interface signature, not a boundary)

**Claim.** The one thing that is genuinely relabel-proof is not a *boundary* but
an *object*: a separator supported on **no proper subset** of the reach. It is
immune to joint-record completion **by construction** — completion works by
admitting more co-present *proper* subsets, and there is no proper subset that
localizes the datum. This is exactly why Lieb-Robinson (a support/light-cone
functional, i.e. a section) cannot relabel it.

**Independent arrivals (D,E,G,A,F,C):** all-or-nothing / AONT / (n,n)
secret-sharing (D: 13,22; C: 34,36,55); nonzero global H¹ class with no local
representative (E: 4; A: 5,2); the only non-local object that survives U1 (G);
non-localizable global-correlation monotone (F: 60,61); primary U4 survivor at
confidence 7 (A: 52).

**Establishes:** the load-bearing deliverable candidate, and the direct structural
heir of the 2026-07-01 precedent — when the "one object, three shadows" *identity
claim* died, the *interface signature* survived; here, when the *physical
boundary* dies, the *global-correlation separator* survives. **Does not
establish** physicality: the separator is an anti-relabel *object*, not a forced
*finality*.

### M4. The Hayden-Preskill adjacency is real but *absorptive*

**Independent arrivals (B,A,G,H,C).** Convergent caution: borrow the

### M5. Transport rung: REDESIGN, not GO (near-unanimous)

Re-scope to: **(iii)** the global-correlation separator (anti-relabel positive) +

**What the convergence does NOT establish.** M1–M6 are one process's six

## Phase 4 — Final Synthesis (the deliverable)

### 1. The repaired problem statement

**Honest headline: the physical-boundary discriminator, stated as a *boundary*,
has no repair that survives in a finite closed model.** M1 is the reason, arrived
at from six independent directions: the joint-record/enlarged-state completion
move can eat any *declared subset* boundary by construction, because in a closed
unitary model every such cut is definable-from-inside. Demanding "physical rather
than declared bookkeeping" *of a boundary* in-house is asking for a definability
artifact to be non-declarable — ill-posed.

**But there is a repair the completion move cannot eat by construction — if we
change the type of the object from a boundary to a separator.** The completion
move operates by admitting co-present **proper** subsets of the reach. A datum
that lives in **no proper subset** has nothing for completion to admit. So:

> **Repaired success criterion (two clauses, separated on purpose).**
>
> **(R1 — the object, construction-proof against completion).** Exhibit two
> capability states such that *no functional of any proper-subset-supported
> statistics of the full co-present register set — observational or
> interventional-within-any-proper-subset — separates them, while a functional
> of the full global joint does.* Because the separator is supported on no proper
> subset, joint-record/enlarged-state completion has no proper subset to admit
> that localizes it, and every support/light-cone (Lieb-Robinson) functional is a
> section blind to it. This clause is **achievable in-house** and is the surviving
> asset; it is an *anti-relabel object*, an interface signature — **not** a
> physical boundary.
>
> **(R2 — the forcing, the unpaid price).** The dynamical step that removes the
> datum from every proper subset must be **derived** from the model's own
> dynamics in a controlled limit (a many-to-one / dissipative / decoupling step,
> earned open-system, large-N, bounded-error) — **not** a stipulated partial
> trace. Only R2 converts R1 from "declared-nonlocal" to "dynamics-nonlocal," and
> only R2 earns the word *physical*. R2 is **not** achievable in a finite closed
> model (M1) and is the open burden.

The split is the whole point. **If you demand R1 ∧ R2, no repair survives
in-house** (the honest finding). **If you accept R1 alone, you have a real but
strictly weaker object** — the relabel-proof separator — which is what actually
survived the gauntlet and what the fallback (§4) is built on. The completion move
"eats" the old criterion precisely because the old criterion was a boundary
(a proper-subset cut); R1 is chosen to be the one shape it cannot eat.

**The linchpin open test (surfaced sharply by G-50, echoed A-52, F).** R1's
guarantee currently rests only on immunity to Lieb-Robinson. The separator must
be shown to resist **all admissible relabelings / re-factorizations of the reach**
— not just support functionals. If some admissible change of tensor factorization
localizes the datum into a proper subset, R1 collapses and the fallback drops to
toolkit-only. *This test, not another fixture, is the true next move.* (See §5.)

### 3. Literature-adjacency verdict

**Borrow, do not collide — and borrow defensively.** The natural home is
Hayden-Preskill / scrambling / decoupling / Petz recovery / Fawzi-Renner CMI, with
the exact dictionary in M4. Specific results to **borrow**: the decoupling theorem
(to *derive* a reach frontier rather than stipulate one), the Petz recovery map
(= the R++ enlargement / section-adjoint already in the artifacts), the
Fawzi-Renner CMI remainder (as the honest *recovery-cost* lower bound — the
correct anti-resource-theory target, a cost payable in reach not work), and the
Yoshida-Kitaev decoder (as an executable recovery probe).

**What to avoid colliding with:** the core HP/complementarity *theorems say the
boundary is recoverable/soft*. Importing them **bounds** finality and **tightens**
the decoherence/resource absorber — so any framing that presents HP as *support*
for a sharp physical boundary is self-refuting. The only residue that is not pure
translation is the **reach-indexing of the recovery superchannel** (a dynamical
resource theory of recovery order); T404 already flagged this as demotable, so
treat it as a thin candidate, not a plan. **Progress test (G-51):** import HP
**only after** the redesigned fixture independently exposes a *reversal-cost*
interface; importing it earlier is authority-laundering.

**GU note (one-way rule, strict).** The firewall-boundary resonance is to be
**flagged, never cited as support** — and it points the *wrong* way: HP implies a
soft boundary, GU asserts a sharp one, so the resonance is anti-support if
anything. GU remains stress-test input only.
