```text

data to globally usable final records, paired with a physical redundancy process
such as Quantum Darwinism / SBS.
```


exists. The threshold is instead:

```text

overlap consistency for eta_F: F -> i aF to support stable global records for a
declared observer capability.
```

  sheaf/descent theory;
