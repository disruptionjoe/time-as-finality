## What This Adds

### 1. Decoherence As The Sampling Engine

Avalanche-style language is useful, but the physical process is not a protocol.
Open-system decoherence already supplies repeated environmental interaction:

```text
weak environmental coupling
  -> partial information leakage
  -> coherence suppression
  -> pointer-state stability
  -> increasing reversal cost
```

This strengthens S4 by replacing a generic consensus analogy with a physical
metastability mechanism.

### 2. Quantum Darwinism As The Bridge

Quantum Darwinism and SBS identify the bridge condition:

```text
redundant, independently accessible, mutually agreeing environmental records of
a pointer observable
```

This is exactly the place where classical-style objectivity appears. It also
explains why classical finality feels consensus-like: observers agree because
the environment has already distributed the same pointer information across
many fragments.

This strengthens S5 but also absorbs overclaim. The bridge is standard physics
unless a future witness shows a TaF distinction after SBS / strong-QD data are
fixed.

### 3. Definite Classical Provenance After The Bridge

S3 should be sharpened:

```text
Classical provenance DAGs become usable after stable pointer records and
environmental redundancy make a Boolean-like event history available.
```

This does not prove that quantum indefinite causal order is generally resolved
by TaF. It suggests a follow-up target:

```text
When do Darwinian records determine a usable classical partial order?
```

That should be treated as a bridge witness, not assumed.

### 4. Thermodynamic Arrow At The Bridge

Finalized records are not free. Amplification, environmental broadcasting,
access, erasure, and maintenance create thermodynamic costs.

The useful TaF reading is:

```text
effective irreversibility enters the record layer when reversal requires
controlling an increasingly large environment.
```

That strengthens the reversal-cost dimension of D1 and the `Lose[K]` side of
the cross-repo effect contract.

This is not new measurement theory. It is a sharper TaF bridge target over
standard decoherence / Quantum Darwinism / SBS.

## Falsification And Demotion

Demote the bridge upgrade if:

- redundancy grows but independent observer agreement does not;
- pointer stability appears without SBS / strong-QD objectivity data;
- classical-style finality appears without redundancy, reversal cost, or
  observer access changes;
- temporal/provenance reconstruction does not improve after the redundancy
  threshold;
- every TaF distinction factors through SBS / strong-QD closure data plus
  observer fragment access.

The last demotion condition is likely and is not a failure of the artifact. It
would classify the idea honestly:

```text
standard physics supplies the bridge engine;
TaF supplies a useful audit vocabulary around access, provenance, and
capability sufficiency.
```
