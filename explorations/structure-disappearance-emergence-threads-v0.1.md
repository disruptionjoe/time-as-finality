## Potential Emerging Theme

A recurring question is appearing:

```text
What happens to structure when it disappears?
```

This question has surfaced through:

- LossKernel
- obstruction relocation
- reconstruction debt
- provenance
- admissibility
- emergence
- observer reconstruction
- scale transitions
- finality

The threads below are attempts to steelman that intuition from multiple
directions. They are not claims. They are preserved as exploratory material for
future persona attention or reduction audits.

## Relation To Previous Preservation Note

[`north-star-persistence-platform-reframing-v0.1.md`](north-star-persistence-platform-reframing-v0.1.md)
preserved the candidate North Star shift:

```text
persistence
  -> preservation
  -> emergence
  -> further emergence
```

The present note adds a second axis:

```text
structure disappears

```

The two axes may be connected. Persistent structures can become platforms for
future emergence, while disappeared structures may survive indirectly as
constraints, debts, or changed admissibility conditions.

relation such as:

```text
Residual(S, f, T, R, O)
```

where:

- `S` is source structure.
- `f` is a transformation, projection, collapse, compression, deletion, or
  forgetting map.
- `T` is target structure.
- `R` is the later reconstruction, admissibility, or operation task.
- `O` is the observer or community maintaining the relevant preservation stack.

The relation would record:

```text
source_record_lost
target_constraint_created
witness_obligation_remaining
admissibility_boundary_changed
future_operation_right_lost_or_created
downstream_structure_enabled_or_blocked
```

This is only a candidate notation. It should be deleted if ordinary
reconstruction debt, provenance, information theory, or coarse-graining already
does the work.

## Reduction Tests

Before any promotion, try to collapse these threads into existing machinery.

### Collapse To Reconstruction Debt

If "what remains" is always an unmet witness obligation under an admissibility
policy, then the right object is reconstruction debt.

### Collapse To Provenance

If "what remains" is always ancestry, custody, authority, or explanation of why
a reconstruction failed, then the right object is provenance or why-not
provenance.

### Collapse To Coarse-Graining

If "what remains" is only a macro-constraint after micro-detail is erased, then
standard coarse-graining may be enough.

### Collapse To Information Theory

If "what remains" can be measured as mutual information, model-count shrinkage,
coding cost, entropy, or rate distortion, then information-theoretic language
should be used directly.

### Collapse To Evolutionary Or Ecological Dynamics

If persistence, repair, inheritance, and platform effects are fully explained
by viability and selection dynamics, then the biology/ecology lane may already
own the object.

### Collapse To Cognitive Science

If the consciousness threads reduce to memory, attention, body ownership,
reportability, social feedback, and predictive processing, then keep them in a
cognitive-science model and do not make TaF-specific claims.

## Non-Collapse Signal

This family of intuitions deserves more attention only if a finite comparison
finds a residual pattern with all of the following:

1. A record or source structure disappears under a declared transformation.
2. A target-side consequence remains and changes a later admissibility,
   reconstruction, authority, or emergence verdict.
3. The target-side consequence is not recoverable from ordinary endpoint state
   alone.
4. The consequence can be classified before seeing the desired conclusion.
5. At least one negative control shows loss without meaningful consequence.
6. At least one domain comparison shows the same structure without relying on
   consciousness, GU, or physics language.

## Failure Modes

Demote or discard this family if:

- "conservation" becomes literal without a conserved quantity and location.
- every disappearance is treated as meaningful by default.
- every persistence interval is called an emergence window.
- the explanation is chosen after seeing what survived.
- consciousness language starts doing evidential work.
- geometry language appears without a defined mathematical object.
- no negative controls exist.
- standard prior art fully explains the examples.
