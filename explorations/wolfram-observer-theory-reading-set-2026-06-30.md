## Why this is the closest neighbor in the reading set

Wolfram Observer Theory and TaF are near-mirror siblings: both **derive observer-facing reality from observer
character**. Wolfram: "the laws of physics are what they are because we are observers of the kind we are."
TaF: observer-facing temporal order and classical objectivity are the stabilization of records across causally
bounded systems. Wolfram's central observer operation -- **equivalencing / coarse-graining** (conflating many
microstates into one: many molecule configurations -> one pressure on a piston) -- is structurally TaF's
**record-finality** (conflating microstates into one finalized fact). This closeness is exactly why the fit
is strong *and* why a distinguishing-predictions statement is now obligatory.

Wolfram's two load-bearing observer properties:
1. **Equivalencing/coarse-graining**, with the deep *open* question: which coarse-grainings are valid? Some
   are "computationally ornate" (huge computation to see two states as equivalent); coarse too finely and you
   "become synonymous with the system."
2. **Belief in a single persistent thread of experience**, which is actually an *aggregate of many branchial
   paths of history* (equivalencing across quantum branches).
Plus **computational boundedness**: the second law holds *because* a bounded observer cannot decrypt the
computationally-irreducible microdynamics. The **ruliad** runs all rules; we sample a *slice* set by our
nature ("rulial reference frames"). Biology video: **the fitness function is the analog of the observer** --
evolution works via computational irreducibility + coarse (observer-like) fitness.

## Two-way discipline (the load-bearing refusal)

Wolfram's "laws because we are observers like us" flirts with observer-creates-reality. TaF's **G1/G3**
guardrails are exactly the discipline Wolfram's framing lacks: observer-relative finality is rendering/access,
not creation. The value is two-way -- Wolfram supplies a fully-developed observer-character -> perceived-law
derivation; TaF supplies the record/causal discipline that keeps it from collapsing into idealism. Map
structure, never merge ontology.
