# G1: Human Belief Does Not Create Matter

## Guardrail

Time as Finality does not claim that human belief creates matter, mass, solidity, gravity, or physical reality.

## Why This Matters

The word "consensus" can be misread as social agreement. In this project, consensus means physical record-consistency across record-bearing systems: instruments, environments, photons, bodies, memories, material constraints, and other traces.

## Unsafe Phrasings

- "Matter is made of agreement."
- "Mass is consensus."
- "A wall is solid because people believe in it."
- "Reality is created by human observers."

## Safer Phrasing

Classical objectivity is tied to stable, redundant, physically propagated records. Human observers participate in that record network, but they do not single-handedly create it.
