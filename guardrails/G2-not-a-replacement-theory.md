# G2: Not A Replacement Theory

## Guardrail

Time as Finality does not replace quantum mechanics, special relativity, general relativity, thermodynamics, proper time, or the technical measurement-problem literature.

This guardrail is a constraint on overclaiming, not a ban on ambitious
formal targets. A claim may move from "not claimed" to "open conjecture" only
when the repo gives it a definition, test path, and failure condition.

## Why This Matters

The framework is currently a conceptual reconstruction project. It may become a useful formalization if it can model record finality without violating known physics. It is not yet a theory.

The correct posture is permissioned ambition:

1. **Not claimed** - unsafe as a result or conclusion.
2. **Open conjecture** - allowed as a research direction with guardrails.
3. **Formal target** - has proposed definitions and a falsification path.
4. **Toy supported** - has bounded executable or mathematical support.
5. **Theory supported** - reproduces or predicts known results under stated
   assumptions.
6. **Rejected or weakened** - failed a test or required narrowing.

## Unsafe Phrasings

- "This explains all of quantum mechanics."
- "This derives general relativity."
- "Proper time is just consensus."
- "Decoherence solves measurement because finality."
- "Black-hole interiors are unreal."
- "Remote galaxies are unrendered until we travel there."
- "Black holes are transportation hubs or render gates."
- "Reality is just a distributed database."
- "TaF has already derived spacetime."
- "TaF has already solved the hard problem of consciousness."
- "TaF replaces thermodynamics as the source of every time arrow."

## Safer Phrasing

The framework asks whether experienced temporal order and classical objectivity can be reconstructed from causally accessible record-stabilization while preserving existing physical constraints.

Ambitious but safer versions:

- Spacetime emergence is an open formal target: derive or refute a
  spacetime-like compatibility structure from finality-domain aggregation.
- The phenomenal bridge is an open formal target: determine whether the gap
  from third-person records to first-person experience is a type gap,
  complexity gap, fixed-point gap, or category-theoretic obstruction.
- Finality-induced temporal direction is an open formal target: separate any
  record-finality direction from thermodynamic entropy increase without
  denying thermodynamics.

For heterodox rendering language, the safer version is:

> Observer-facing reality may be modeled as locally consistent record histories that reconcile where causal domains overlap, while received remote signals still count as real causal records and horizons remain causal-access boundaries rather than travel mechanisms.
