# G3: Observer Rendering Is Not Mind-Created Matter

## Guardrail

Observer-rendering language does not mean consciousness creates matter, spacetime, physical law, or other people.

## Why This Matters

The observer-renderer frame is useful only if it keeps three layers distinct:

- what exists or constrains interaction;
- what becomes accessible to an observer through records;
- what is experienced as a rendered, action-guiding world.

Collapsing those layers turns a potentially useful observer-finality model into naive idealism or solipsism.

## Unsafe Phrasings

- "Matter exists because we agree it exists."
- "My consciousness creates spacetime."
- "Other people are just objects in my render."
- "The body is fake."
- "Rendered means arbitrary or rewriteable by will."
- "The universe starts when I am born."

## Safer Phrasing

Observer rendering means:

> A conscious observer experiences an action-guiding world through accessible records and constraints. The body is a high-finality participation interface inside that rendered world, not proof that matter is imaginary. Other observers may be independently real while remaining directly inaccessible except through traces, interaction, and shared records.
