## The tight hook (why this is a type-check, not a loose analogy)

The pre-check found the precise pivot. **Mostafazadeh (pseudo-Hermitian QM):** *the
choice of an irreducible family of observables fixes the metric operator* (the
physical inner product), up to a factor. **TaF:** the admissible menu / observable
algebra `⟨M_R⟩` fixes whether the separating datum is inside or outside the
physical/accessible structure (declared vs forced). **Both say: the observable
algebra determines the physical structure.** That is a concrete, checkable
correspondence — the adapter's job is to see whether it is a genuine functor or
merely rhymes.
