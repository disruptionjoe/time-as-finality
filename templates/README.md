# Templates

Copy-ready file shapes for contributions, extracted verbatim from the shapes
documented in [`../CONTRIBUTING.md`](../CONTRIBUTING.md). These are scaffolding
only — they hold no claims and assert no research truth.

| Template | Use for | Route to |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `claim-file-template.md` | A new typed claim. | `claims/` (or `CLAIM-LEDGER.md` only after an artifact runs and earns it). |
| `test-file-template.md` | A new toy model / test. | `tests/`. Treat the `T#` as a placeholder until checked against `TESTS.md` and `tests/`. |

Promotion gates and the agent-driven exploration protocol live in
`CONTRIBUTING.md`; nothing here overrides them. No new claim, physics, geometry,
or novelty upgrade until the proposed artifact runs and yields a narrow theorem,
separation, or falsifying result. Preserve negative results and demotions as
first-class progress.
