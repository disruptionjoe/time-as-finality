# T148: H7 Paper-Facing Demotion Gate

## Route

Thermodynamic arrow of time.

## Question

After T145, should H7 still be described in paper-facing text as a
partially-supported physical-arrow claim, or only as a conditional constructor
and resource-accounting audit discipline?

## Motivation

T18 proves a finite constructor-style theorem under D1-monotone admissibility.
The later obstruction stack blocks the physical reading:

- closed finite reversible dynamics do not support a strict scalar finality
  monotone;
- open and stationary stochastic fixtures are absorbed by path
  irreversibility, exported history, sink state, or capacity accounting;
- the explicit T1 record graph has no constructor-impossible reverse edge; and
- T145 finds that fixed-accounting deletion topology is future-operation
  residue, not arrow evidence.

The missing artifact is a status gate that prevents the T18 formal theorem
from being cited as physics support after these absorbers.

## Setup

T148 classifies the current H7 evidence profile:

```text
conditional constructor theorem = present
closed reversible obstruction = present
open stochastic absorption = present
record-graph reverse grounded = false
fixed-accounting deletion candidate = false
fixed-accounting capability residue = true
```

The output is a paper-facing status, allowed claim list, rejected claim list,
open blocker list, and reinstatement condition.

## Success Criteria

- Preserve T18 as a legitimate conditional constructor theorem.
- Demote H7 as a physical-arrow or thermodynamic-arrow claim.
- Preserve the T145 topology/future-operation residue without treating it as
  arrow evidence.
- State the exact reinstatement gate: a physically typed deletion substrate
  whose reverse remains constructor-impossible after full absorber accounting.

## Failure Criteria

- Deleting the conditional constructor theorem rather than demoting its
  physical reading.
- Treating fixed-erasure topology residue as thermodynamic-arrow evidence.
- Allowing H7 paper-facing physical-arrow language without the T145 gate.

## Claim Impact

H7 should be paper-facing only as:

```text
a conditional constructor/resource-accounting audit discipline
```

not as a supported physical-arrow or thermodynamic-arrow claim. Reopen the
physical reading only if a substrate-native physical deletion reverse remains
constructor-impossible after free-energy, capacity, sink, observer-boundary,
provenance, source-copy, and reversible-control data are matched.
