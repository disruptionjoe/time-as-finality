## Central Question

Can the actual T54 canonical quotient-union completions be sent directly
through the T126 causal-set screen after the T58 gap-phantom well-formedness
gate, or were T126's S1 dependencies still mostly synthetic?

## Hypothesis Under Audit

The T54 canonical T51/T52 completions should reach the finite causal-set gate,
but they should not become spacetime-facing witnesses unless they also clear
the T126 manifoldlikeness and dimension-diagnostic scale requirements.

## Setup

T154 uses existing artifacts only:

```text
T54 canonical completion
  -> T58 phantom-gap exact-match gate
  -> T126 FinalityColimitCausetDatum
  -> causal-set and manifoldlikeness screen
```

The bridge audits:

- `T51_phantom_repair`;
- `T52_symmetric_reconstruction`;
- `T53_ambiguous_identity` as a boundary that must not become embeddability
  evidence.

## Success Criteria

- T51 and T52 are adapted from T54 completions, not manually recreated.
- T58 exact-match extension is required before phantom gaps are treated as
  resolved.
- T51 and T52 reach T126 as finite causal-set candidates.
- If they are too small for T126's manifold filter, the result is a weakening,
  not an upgrade.
- T53 is blocked before any causal-set claim.

## Failure Criteria

T154 fails if:

- it duplicates T54/T58 fixture data instead of using the executable results;
- it lets T53-style underdetermined data enter embeddability screening;
- it treats a three- or four-event causal-set candidate as spacetime-like;
- it applies a named dimension estimator below the declared scale threshold;
- it strengthens S1 without a larger colimit, dimension diagnostic,
  sprinkling/locality check, or continuum-limit bridge.

## Known Physics Constraints

Passing this bridge does not derive spacetime, Lorentzian geometry, metric
structure, GR, QFT, or a continuum limit. It only says whether existing finite
finality colimits survive the next necessary-condition screen.

## Implementation Result

Status: implemented.

T154 adapts T54's canonical T51/T52 completions into T126 datums after the T58
well-formed phantom-gap gate. Both completions become finite causal-set
candidates, but both are classified `insufficient_scale`. The Myrheim-Meyer
ordering-fraction diagnostic is recorded only as a readiness boundary, not
applied as a dimension estimate.

The strongest result is negative:

```text
T51/T52 are causal-set gate controls, not spacetime-reconstruction witnesses.
```
