# T452: Law-Sector Nonadmissibility Gate

## Target Claims

- [Region-Indexed Capability Discriminator](../open-problems/region-indexed-capability-discriminator.md)
- T445 substrate-law packet calibration
- T434 substrate-law admission gate
- T436/T447 resource-lift guardrail posture

## Setup

T445 cleared the T434 admission shape: a finite Z2 law packet derived the
revision edge from a predeclared conservation rule rather than a transition
table. It still failed to become a discriminator success because explicit
law-sector completion restored factorization.

T452 makes the next burden executable. A future stronger packet must not merely
hide the law sector from region `R`; it must show that the named law-sector
completion is physically non-admissible under a frozen operation/resource class.

## Admission Criteria

A packet is admitted only if it:

- clears T434 law admission first;
- matches all declared `R`-supported statistics and interventions;
- splits capability only under the boundary/law menu;
- names the law-sector completion it blocks;
- freezes the allowed operation/resource class before pair selection;
- audits A2 reference/resource completion;
- supplies a predeclared exact nonadmissibility witness;
- includes a negative control;
- avoids hidden labels, post-hoc policies, and transition-table restatements.

## Success Criteria

- The current T445 Z2 law packet is rejected as
  `NOT_ADMITTED_COMPLETION_MERELY_OUTSIDE_R`.
- T445-style reference/resource lift controls remain rejected or absorbed.
- Transition-table, post-hoc, hidden-label, missing-A2, no-witness, cost-only,
  and no-negative-control packets are rejected.
- A synthetic fully specified packet is admitted only as a future review target,
  with no promotion language.
- The result stays recorded-tier with no claim-ledger, roadmap, public posture,
  North Star, hard-policy, or cross-repo movement.

## Failure Criteria

The artifact fails if:

- it admits the current T445 packet as a discriminator success;
- it treats a law sector that is merely outside `R` as physically non-admissible;
- it skips the A2 resource-lift audit;
- it counts high cost or family-limit burden as this single-packet gate;
- it admits transition-table, post-hoc, hidden-label, or missing-control packets;
- it claims a physics theorem, WAY theorem, claim promotion, or public posture
  movement.

## Contribution Needed

Use T452 as the intake gate for any next Direction-A law-sector packet. The
stronger route is not "hide the compensator better"; it is "freeze a physical
operation/resource class and prove the compensator completion is unavailable
there."
