- [Region-Indexed Capability Discriminator](../open-problems/region-indexed-capability-discriminator.md)
- T454 integrated E3-region packet swing
- T455 T454 hostile-review gate

## Setup

T456 rejects current T454/T455 for stronger Direction-A posture because the
packet still misses description nonfactorization, reference-policy invariance or
preclusion, and a policy-independent region theorem. T457 sharpens the first
blocker. T458 focuses on the second blocker.

The current integrated E3-region packet has a positive finite non-wrapping
exact-catalyst policy, but cyclic, consumed, and ideal-reference policies route
away from that finite exact packet. A stronger packet must either preserve the
boundary split across those variants or preclude them by a policy-independent
theorem declared before pair selection.

## Success Criteria

- Current T454/T455/T457 is rejected as reference-policy relative.
- Finite-policy-only selection is rejected as incomplete scope.
- Cyclic, consumed, and ideal reference policies are treated as required variants
  to handle or independently preclude.
- Post-hoc scope, post-hoc preclusion, policy-dependent preclusion, hidden-label
  shortcuts, and missing controls are rejected.
- Only synthetic invariant or independently precluded future targets are
  admitted, with no claim promotion.

## Failure Criteria

The gate fails if it:

- treats current T454/T455/T457 as admitted for stronger Direction-A posture;
- treats finite non-wrapping policy selection as enough;
- precludes cyclic, consumed, or ideal references by declaration only;
- ignores post-hoc or hidden-label reference-policy selection;
- edits `CLAIM-LEDGER.md`, `TESTS.md`, `ROADMAP.md`, README, North Star files,
  public posture, hard policy, or cross-repo truth.

## Known Physics Constraints

T458 is a repo-local admission/guardrail gate over finite toy packet classes. It
is not a WAY theorem, not a quantum physics claim, not a real substrate law, not
cross-repo support, and not claim-ledger movement.

## Contribution Needed

Use T458 to stop reading the current finite non-wrapping exact-catalyst packet as
reference-policy stable. Future Direction-A packets must either preserve the
split across cyclic, consumed, and ideal reference variants or independently
preclude those variants before pair selection. If no such packet can be supplied,
the integrated E3-region route should demote to a useful negative guardrail after
the theorem-supply blocker is also audited.
