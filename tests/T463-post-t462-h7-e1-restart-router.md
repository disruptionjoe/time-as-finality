- treats E1/E2/E3 packets as H7 restarts without their mode gates;
- admits access loss, provenance loss, sector/gauge relabeling, finite

  promotion shortcuts;
- edits `CLAIM-LEDGER.md`, `TESTS.md`, `ROADMAP.md`, README, North Star files,
  public posture, hard policy, or cross-repo truth.
