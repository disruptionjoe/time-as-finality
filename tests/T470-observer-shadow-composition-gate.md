2. Endpoint-only transport comparison preserves a verdict despite different
   accumulated forgotten structure.
3. LossKernel canonical neighbor factoring fails to preserve the obligation.
4. The result claims an observer-shadow category theorem, North Star geometry
   proof, or claim-ledger movement.
