## Question

Can T472-admitted observer-shadow packets compose in finite controls without
turning indexed bookkeeping, absorber completion, or cross-family jumps into an
observer-shadow category theorem?

## Setup

T473 has two parts.

First, it builds a three-step typed transport fixture and compares the current
D1 morphism-composition core under left and right association, ignoring only
composition-generated names. It also compares the accumulated forgotten-structure
index under the two associations.

Second, it composes T472 route atoms:

- repeated path-indexed transport bookkeeping;
- repeated LossKernel neighbor-visible preservation controls;
- LossKernel preservation with hidden-source absorber completion;
- endpoint-only transport after T472 rejection;
- cross-family transport/LossKernel composition with no declared bridge.

## Success Criteria

T473 succeeds if it:

- shows the finite three-step transport fixture is associative up to structural
  morphism fields and forgotten-index accumulation;
- treats repeated path-indexed transport as associative bookkeeping only;
- treats repeated LossKernel preservation as a finite preservation control only;
- routes hidden-source completion to absorber/state-completion bookkeeping;
- blocks any composition containing a T472-rejected packet;
- blocks cross-family composition unless a bridge is declared;
- leaves claim status, public posture, North Star, roadmap, README, hard policy,
  and cross-repo truth untouched.

## Failure Criteria

T473 fails if it:

- claims a global category, indexed-category, or fibration theorem;
- treats absorber completion as category evidence;
- composes rejected T472 packets;
- composes transport and LossKernel packets without a declared bridge;
- promotes D1, PO1, TF1, LossKernel, observer-shadow category, North Star
  geometry, or any physics/consciousness claim.

## Known Physics Constraints

None. T473 is a finite bookkeeping/admission gate. It does not make physics,
consciousness, or observer-metaphysics claims.
