## Question

What counts as an explicit bridge for composing unlike observer-shadow packet
families, after T473 rejects transport/LossKernel composition with no declared
bridge?

## Setup

T474 consumes T473 route cases and builds bridge controls:

- typed-transport indexed-bookkeeping positive control;
- LossKernel preservation positive control;

- upstream T472-rejection hostile control;
- absorber-completion hostile control.

It then evaluates five bridge proposals:

- no declared bridge;
- semantic keyword bridge;
- absorber-completion bridge;
- direct category bridge shortcut;
- audit-atlas bridge.

## Success Criteria

T474 succeeds if it:

- rejects no-bridge cross-family composition;
- rejects semantic relabeling as a bridge;
- rejects absorber completion as category evidence;
- rejects direct category/fibration shortcuts;
- admits at most an audit-atlas bridge that keeps family-specific shadows,
  capability objects, and native comparisons separate;
- leaves claim status, public posture, North Star, roadmap, README, hard policy,
  and cross-repo truth untouched.

## Failure Criteria

T474 fails if it:

- treats semantic similarity as a typed bridge;
- uses hidden-source completion as cross-family category evidence;
- identifies PO1 admissibility and LossKernel obligations as one capability
  object without a proof;
- claims an observer-shadow category, indexed-category, or fibration theorem;
- promotes D1, PO1, TF1, LossKernel, North Star geometry, or any
  physics/consciousness claim.

## Known Physics Constraints

None. T474 is a finite bridge-admission guardrail. It does not make physics,
consciousness, or observer-metaphysics claims.
