## Success Criteria

T491 succeeds if it:

- treats T490 as review admission only;
- rejects rerunning the uniform finite ordinal baseline after T223;
- rejects survivor-tail indicators and screen-stack conditioning as circular or
  diagnostic;
- rejects post-T223 screen drift and single-size positives;
- blocks S1 promotion, T223 reversal, spacetime derivation, manifoldlikeness,
  dimension, sprinkling, Lorentzian metric, continuum theorem, public posture,
  claim status, and external-action shortcuts;
- admits only future review targets with an independent measure law, a
  continuum bridge, or a separate formal entry point;
- keeps admitted future targets as review targets, not S1 evidence;
- leaves claim status, public posture, North Star, roadmap, README, hard policy,
  protected license, physics posture, and cross-repo truth untouched.

## Failure Criteria

T491 fails if it:

- treats T490 admission as S1 evidence;
- reopens uniform, tail-tuned, screen-conditioned, screen-drift, or single-size
  finite-route variants;
- moves claim status, public posture, roadmap, README, North Star, hard policy,
  protected license, or cross-repo truth;
- claims spacetime derivation, manifoldlikeness, dimension, sprinkling law,
  Lorentzian metric, locality, covariance, embedding, continuum theorem, GR, or
  QFT evidence.

## Known Physics Constraints

T491 is not a physics result. It does not evaluate Lorentzian geometry, GR, QFT,
random sprinkling, locality, covariance, embedding, topos semantics, NCG,
path-integral dynamics, stochastic geometry, or continuum limits. It only closes
minor finite-route restarts and preserves review paths for separate evidence.
