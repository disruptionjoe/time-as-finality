# T505: Observer/System Gauge Reality Gate

## Target Claims

- `open-problems/boundary-adapter-as-functor-spec-2026-07-07.md`
- `explorations/physics-from-a-finite-observer-and-the-observer-system-gauge-2026-07-07.md`
- CT-3 correction: unobservable does not mean gauge unless equivalence is proved
- T504 boundary-adapter source-category functor gate

## Setup

Build a finite classifier for packets that try to route the observer/system
split or the current two-adapter agreement. The fixtures include:

- a true gauge/equivalence calibration with a proved reversible translation;
- a no-observable-difference-only shortcut;
- an unobservable but hard-to-vary hidden-structure calibration;
- the current two-adapter state, with two forcing sources but one process and
  one D1 encoding;
- a future de-correlated two-adapter packet;
- constant/trivial convergence and shared-knob controls;
- missing hard-to-vary-burden and external/cross-repo shortcut controls.

## Success Criteria

- Proven equivalence is routed to invariant-only treatment.
- Lack of direct observability alone is rejected as a gauge shortcut.
- Hard-to-vary, falsifiable hidden structure is admitted only as a review
  target and not as claim evidence.
- The current two-adapter state is admitted only as single-process
  independent-forcing review material, with de-correlation still pending.
- A future packet with independent forcing, no shared knob, no shared brief,
  falsifiable controls, and a declared capability bound is admitted only for
  review.

## Failure Criteria

- Constant or trivial maps are rejected.
- Shared tunable observer/system knobs are rejected as manufactured
  convergence.
- Missing hard-to-vary explanations or missing falsifiable disagreement
  controls are rejected.
- Claim, public-posture, external-publication, and cross-repo shortcuts are
  blocked.

## Known Physics Constraints

This is not a physics theorem and not a GU/TI/TaF identity result. It is a
repo-local guard that prevents two opposite overreads: collapsing unobservable
structure to gauge, and treating hard-to-vary explanatory structure as already
proved. No sibling repository is inspected or modified.

## Contribution Needed

Future de-correlation work must fix the observer/system split independently on
each adapter, rule out a shared tunable knob or shared encoding brief, include
falsifiable disagreement controls, declare the finite capability bound, and
keep the packet review-only until a runnable artifact earns a narrower update.
