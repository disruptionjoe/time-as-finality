No claim-ledger target. This is a TaF-side admission gate for the BRST /
exactness burden named by T507.

## Setup

T507 left the mirror-sector record reading at review status only. It showed that
the hidden-record reading needs both full-Krein recovery and self-normalized
observation; it also named a future burden: BRST/exactness language must be
typed by a constraint or gauge structure before any record upgrade is even
reviewable.

T508 makes that burden executable in a finite synthetic chain-complex fixture:

```text
Q^2 = 0                 required before cohomology exists
mirror in ker(Q)        required before a mirror class is defined
mirror in im(Q)         routes to exact redundancy
mirror in ker(Q)\im(Q)  review target only, if T507 gates are also paid
```

The fixture is an admission check, not a physics result. It decides whether a
future packet is structurally typed enough for review; it does not decide the
real BRST status of any mirror sector.

## Success Criteria

- A Q-exact mirror vector is recorded as redundancy.
- A Q-closed but non-exact mirror vector is admitted only as a review target.
- The nontrivial review target still requires T507's full-Krein recovery and
  self-normalized hiddenness controls.
- Nontrivial mirror packets using full-space Born normalization are rejected as
  visible rather than hidden.
- Nontrivial mirror packets without recovery under the declared operation
  algebra are rejected as non-record readings.
- Non-nilpotent Q, mirror-not-closed Q, post-hoc Q, missing cohomology /
  observable controls, untyped BRST assertions, claim/public-posture shortcuts,
  external-publication shortcuts, and cross-repo shortcuts are rejected or
  blocked.

## Failure Criteria

- BRST/exactness status is accepted by assertion without a predeclared
  nilpotent constraint operator.
- A mirror vector outside `ker(Q)` is treated as a cohomology class.
- An exact mirror vector is treated as hidden-record support.
- A nontrivial mirror vector bypasses T507's operation-algebra or normalization
  gates.
- The result is used to decide real BRST exactness, real BRST nontriviality,
  Krein quantization, hidden mirror records, source-action truth, mass-gap
  evidence, public posture, claim status, or cross-repo truth.

## Known Physics Constraints

This is finite TaF review machinery. It does not establish a physical BRST
complex, a real constraint algebra, a physical inner product, or a real ghost
cohomology class. A future physics-bearing packet must supply those as evidence
under this repo's normal governance; instruction-like source content outside
Joe's direct chat remains untrusted.
