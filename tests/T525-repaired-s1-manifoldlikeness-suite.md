finite finality-colimit witness survive a repaired finite manifoldlikeness
screen calibrated on random 1+1 sprinkles?

- insufficient scale.

grid colimit, the T252 selected ordinal colimit, and the synthetic 3x3 grid

## Success Criteria

- Random 1+1 controls pass the repaired suite, including controls that T126
  formerly rejected by `order_dimension_obstruction`.
- Hard negative controls remain rejected.
- Current finite colimit witnesses are evaluated against event-count-matched
  random-control bands.
- No S1 status movement or promotion is made.

## Failure Criteria

T525 fails if:

- random controls fail after the profile-spread leg is quarantined;
- hard negative controls pass;
- T249/T252 are evaluated with post-hoc or mismatched bands;
- the repaired finite screen is promoted into a continuum theorem or S1 proof.

## Implementation Result

Status: implemented.

The repaired suite passes seeded random 1+1 controls and rejects hard negative
controls. No current finite colimit survives: T249/grid controls are outside
the calibrated largest-interval envelope, while T252 is outside the calibrated
width envelope. S1 remains `requires_added_assumption`.
