## Central Question

After T525, is the live S1 blocker the repaired manifoldlikeness suite itself,
or the lack of a finality-native source law that can produce repaired-suite
survivors without importing Lorentzian coordinates?

## Setup

T526 audits the external seeded 1+1 causal-diamond random law:

```text
sample independent u,v light-cone coordinates;
order p < q iff u_p < u_q and v_p < v_q.
```

This law is predeclared and passes the repaired T525 finite suite by design as
a calibration reference. It is not TaF-native: the u/v coordinates are the
target Lorentzian structure, not output from finality-domain colimit data.

The audit compares five packet classes:

- external 1+1 causal-diamond reference law;
- current T249/T252 finite finality-colimit route;
- screen-conditioned survivor law;
- Lorentzian-import promotion shortcut;
- synthetic future TaF-native generator/bridge target.

## Success Criteria

- The external reference-law samples pass the repaired T525 suite.
- Current finite colimits still have zero repaired-suite survivors.
- The external reference law is classified as calibration-only, not S1
  evidence.
- Screen-conditioned and promotion shortcut packets are rejected or blocked.
- A future TaF-native generator/bridge packet is admitted only as review target.

## Failure Criteria

T526 fails if:

- the external reference law does not pass the repaired suite;
- current finite colimits survive the repaired suite unexpectedly;
- imported Lorentzian coordinates are counted as S1 evidence;
- a screen-conditioned law is admitted;
- the result promotes S1, reverses T223, or claims spacetime derivation.
