## Central Question

After T532 rejects the first receipt-product starter candidates and blocks
target import, screen conditioning, and repaired-band fitting, is there any
credible new finality-domain law with an independent comparability-density
reason, or should the S1 finite-generator route pause until a new source law
exists?

## Predeclared Candidate Menu

T534 excludes the already-tested or forbidden shapes from the candidate menu:

- two-channel receipt product order;
- three-channel receipt product order;
- Lorentzian `u/v` reference import;
- T528/T530 screen-conditioned receipt mixture;
- post-hoc repaired-band fitting.

The one new candidate evaluated here is:

```text
record_window_separation_order
```

Each record has an open endpoint and a finalized endpoint in a finality-domain
window. For two records `x` and `y`, declare `x < y` only when `x` finalizes
before `y` opens. The candidate is independent of Lorentzian coordinates and
does not tune against the T528/T530 screen.

## Success Criteria

- The computation is deterministic and exits 0.
- The candidate menu excludes the T532-negative and forbidden shapes.
- At least one genuinely new finality-domain law candidate is evaluated.
- Candidate and route outcomes are classified only as `CLEARED`, `NARROWED`,
  `FALSIFIED`, `PAUSE`, or `REVIEW_ONLY`, with reasons.
- If no new law clears, the result states that difficulty alone is not
  falsification, but the finite-generator route should pause until a new source
  law exists.
- The writeup labels claims as `COMPUTED` or `ARGUED` with confidence.
- No S1 promotion, claim/canon movement, public posture movement, external
  publication, or cross-repo truth movement is made.

## Failure Criteria

T534 fails if:

- it reintroduces the two-channel or three-channel receipt-product candidates
  as candidate-menu items;
- it imports Lorentzian `u/v` coordinates;
- it conditions the law on the T528/T530 screen;
- it fits a post-hoc repaired band;
- it treats a new but density-mismatched law as S1 evidence;
- it demotes the North Star merely because the route is difficult;
- it moves S1, claim status, Canon Index tiers, canon verdicts, public posture,
  external-publication state, or cross-repo truth.
