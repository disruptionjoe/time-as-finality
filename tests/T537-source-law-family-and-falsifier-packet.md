# T537: Source-Law Family And Falsifier Packet

## Target Claims

- TAF11: North Star source-law reassessment beyond the finite-generator route.
- [S1: Spacetime As Consensus Envelope](../claims/S1-spacetime-consensus-envelope.md)
- [T534: New Finality-Domain Law Pause Router](T534-new-finality-domain-law-pause-router.md)
- [T536: TAF11 Source-Law Successor Router](T536-taf11-source-law-successor-router.md)

## Central Question

Given T536's selected route, which new finality-domain source-law family should
be computed next, and what falsifier must be attached before computation reads
any target ordering statistic or Lorentzian reference?

T537 is not another successor router. It is the bounded family-selection packet
that T536 selected.

## Candidate Family Shapes

T537 evaluates:

- a descent-obstruction resolution family over finite record covers;
- a stabilization-certificate filtration family;
- a rescue of the spent record-window endpoint shape;
- a target-density-fitting family;
- an external Lorentzian reference sampler;
- a Track-2 data-packet wait as a Track-1 replacement.

## Success Criteria

- The computation is deterministic and exits 0.
- T536 is consumed as selecting the T537 route.
- Exactly one next source-law family is selected for execution.
- The selected family is finality-domain native, new relative to spent shapes,
  computable without target import, and has a predeclared falsifier.
- Spent shapes, target-density fitting, Lorentzian reference import, and Track-2
  replacement are rejected or paused.
- No S1, claim-ledger, Canon Index, canon-verdict, public-posture,
  external-publication, or cross-repo truth movement occurs.

## Failure Criteria

T537 fails if:

- it selects more than one next family;
- it repeats two-channel, three-channel, or record-window spent shapes;
- it chooses the law from the target ordering statistic;
- it imports Lorentzian coordinates as the source law;
- it lets Track 2 replace Track 1 without a real data-bearing packet;
- it moves S1, claim status, Canon Index tiers, canon verdicts, public posture,
  external-publication state, or cross-repo truth.

python -m models.t537_source_law_family_and_falsifier_packet --write-results

## Boundary

T537 selects a source-law family and next computation contract only. It does not
establish a source law, prove S1, or update any governance or public posture
surface.
