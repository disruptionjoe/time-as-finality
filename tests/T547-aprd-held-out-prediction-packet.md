## Purpose

T546 found finite APRD functoriality across native morphisms and composites.
T547 tests whether that frozen APRD object can predict held-out native fixture
debt before target outcome labels are read or the rule is retuned.

The packet must pass:

- a native complete fixture whose predicted debt is empty;
- native debt-bearing record-transport, T51/T58, and T19 fixtures;
- validation that predicted APRD debt equals the revealed held-out label.

The packet must reject:

- outcome-label leakage;
- proxy-label reading;
- post-hoc retuning;
- hidden support change;
- non-native held-out fixtures;
- finite prediction read as source-law status.

## Success Criteria

1. T547 consumes the T546 APRD functoriality verdict.
2. The frozen predictor reads required and observed support before target label
   reveal.
3. A native clear fixture predicts no APRD debt.
4. Native debt-bearing fixtures predict their APRD debt sets.
5. Every accepted prediction matches the revealed held-out label.
6. Leakage, proxy, retuning, hidden-support, non-native, and source-law
   overclaim controls reject.
7. No claim, canon, public-posture, North Star, external-publication, or
   cross-repo movement is made.

## Expected Result

`aprd_held_out_prediction_gate_clears_native_fixtures`.

T547 should keep APRD alive as a finite source-law feeder, not a source law. The
next useful packet should stress the frozen predictor on a distinct native
