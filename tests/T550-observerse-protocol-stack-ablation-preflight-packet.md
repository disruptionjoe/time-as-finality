# T550: Observerse Protocol-Stack Ablation Preflight Packet

## Target Claims

- S1 guarded context
- TAF11 source-law reassessment after APRD narrowing
- T549 selected route: `observerse_protocol_stack_ablation_preflight`
- T527 Observerse protocol-stack ablation harness as review-only source evidence

## Central Question

Can the T549-selected Observerse protocol-stack route be turned into a bounded
preflight contract that freezes the layers, source variables, collapse-mode
predictions, hostile controls, and next executable stress packet before any
new outcome reading?

## Setup

T550 consumes:

- T549 as route authority: the post-APRD TAF11 router selected exactly one
  next route, `observerse_protocol_stack_ablation_preflight`;
- T527 as frozen illustration-grade evidence: issuance, admissibility,
  sybil/finality, consensus, and near-term governance removals collapse
  sustained coherent structure in the deterministic Observerse harness.

The preflight freezes five layer contracts:

- issuance -> freeze;
- admissibility -> incoherence;
- sybil/finality -> capture;
- consensus -> fragment;
- governance -> ossification under near-term rules.

Governance remains conditional: it is load-bearing under near-term fixed rules,
but the T527 full-horizon-rule control still shows that governance is not an
unconditional fifth primitive.

## Success Criteria

- T549 route authority is intact.
- T527 remains review-only and does not become validation.
- Exactly five near-term protocol-stack layers are predeclared.
- Each layer has a source variable, collapse-mode prediction, source-law
  burden, and predeclared signal.
- The frozen T527 collapse map matches the preflight contract.
- Hostile controls block post-hoc layer choice, APRD retuning, cross-repo truth
  import, TAF4/source-law shortcuts, and claim/canon/public-posture movement.
- A single next packet is named for a later executable stress test.

## Failure Criteria

T550 fails if:

- the packet treats T527 as Observerse validation, S1 evidence, or source-law
  proof;
- layers are chosen after target outcomes are read;
- governance is either dropped unconditionally or promoted as unconditional;
- APRD is retuned or replayed as the protocol-stack rule;
- cross-repo truth, Lorentzian target structure, or outcome labels are imported;
- TAF4, source-law status, claim rows, Canon Index tiers, canon verdicts,
  public posture, external publication, or cross-repo truth is moved.

## Implementation Result

Status: implemented.

T550 builds the review-only preflight contract. The five layer contracts are
frozen, all preflight gates pass, and the next executable packet is
`t551_observerse_protocol_stack_source_law_stress_packet`.

T550 does not validate Observerse, establish a source law, prove a
shadow-protection theorem, move TAF4, execute TAF8, or change claim/canon/public
posture.

## Boundary

T550 is a preflight contract only. It authorizes a later stress packet shape; it
does not supply evidence strong enough for claim-ledger, Canon Index,
source-law, public-posture, or finite-to-continuum movement.
