# T554: Observerse Protocol-Stack Minimality Gate

## Target Claims

S1, TAF11, TAF4, TAF8, T553, T552, T551, T550.

## Setup

Consume T553 as boundary authority. The admitted bounded-native class is:

- T551 bounded native finality fixture
- T552 independent handoff/partition/repair transfer fixture
- one phase-rotated native fixture shape

The frozen layer set remains unchanged:

- issuance
- admissibility
- sybil/finality
- consensus
- governance

## Success Criteria

- T553 selected T554.
- The admitted class remains T551, T552, and one phase-rotated native fixture.
- The complete frozen stack rescues every admitted fixture.
- Dropping issuance, admissibility, sybil/finality, consensus, or near-term
  governance produces the expected collapse mode: freeze, incoherence, capture,
  fragment, or ossification.
- Full-horizon precomputed rules rescue without governance, preserving the
  conditional-governance boundary.
- The phase-rotated fixture is not a replay of T551 or T552.
- The next packet is absorber separation, not source-law, TAF4, TAF8,
  claim-ledger, public-posture, or cross-repo movement.

## Failure Criteria

T554 fails if:

- any frozen layer is renamed, dropped, or silently changed;
- any admitted fixture still rescues after a single-layer near-term drop;
- governance is made unconditional or deleted without the full-horizon control;
- the third fixture reuses T551 or T552 records;
- bounded-class minimality is promoted to source-law status;
- claim rows, Canon Index tiers, canon verdicts, public posture, TAF4, TAF8,
  external publication, or cross-repo truth are moved.

## Known Physics Constraints

No physics, geometry, causal-set, Lorentzian, quantum, or source-law claim is
earned by this gate.

## Implementation Result

Status: implemented.

T554 finds that every frozen layer is load-bearing across the admitted
bounded-native class. Complete stack rescues T551, T552, and the phase-rotated
fixture; single-layer removals produce the expected failure modes. Governance
remains conditional because full-horizon rules still rescue.

T554 selects `t555_observerse_protocol_stack_absorber_separation_gate` as the
next TAF11 gate. It does not validate Observerse, establish a source law, move
