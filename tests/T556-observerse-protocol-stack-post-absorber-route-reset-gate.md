# T556: Observerse Protocol-Stack Post-Absorber Route Reset Gate

## Target Claims

S1, TAF11, TAF4, TAF8, T555, T554, T553, T552, T551, T550.

## Setup

Consume T555 as absorber-completion authority:

- mature protocol/state-machine, consensus/distributed-systems, governance, and
  record-provenance absorbers received their normal state and comparison rights;
- the strong Observerse source-law reading was absorbed after same-neighbor-data
  completion;
- the remaining value of the T550-T555 protocol-stack route is audit
  translation or heuristic residue only;
- T555 selected
  `t556_observerse_protocol_stack_post_absorber_route_reset_gate`.

Evaluate the post-absorber reset routes:

- park the absorbed Observerse protocol-stack route as audit translation
  residue and require a fresh TAF11 source-law family preflight;
- reject more bounded Observerse fixtures as source-law evidence;
- pause immediate fresh-family selection until a family, source variables,
  absorbers, and falsifiers are declared;
- block TAF4, TAF8, claim-ledger, Canon Index, public-posture, publication, and
  cross-repo shortcuts.

## Success Criteria

- T555 is consumed as the source state.
- Exactly one route reset is selected.
- The selected reset parks the Observerse protocol-stack route rather than
  extending it.
- The route preserves audit translation value without treating the absorbed
  route as source-law evidence.
- Immediate fresh-family selection pauses unless a source family, variables,
  absorbers, and falsifiers are predeclared.
- TAF4 finite-to-continuum movement and TAF8 cross-domain execution are blocked.
- No claim-ledger, Canon Index, canon-verdict, public-posture, North Star,
  external-publication, or cross-repo movement is made.

## Failure Criteria

T556 fails if:

- T555 is not the source authority;
- another Observerse protocol-stack fixture is selected as if fixture count
  defeats absorber completion;
- a fresh source-law family is selected without predeclared absorbers and
  falsifiers;
- audit translation residue is promoted to source-law, claim, canon, or public
  posture;
- TAF4, TAF8, external publication, or cross-repo truth is moved.

## Known Physics Constraints

No physics, geometry, causal-set, Lorentzian, quantum, source-law, or
shadow-protection claim is earned by this gate.

## Implementation Result

Status: implemented.

T556 consumes T555 as absorber-completion authority and parks the T550-T555
Observerse protocol-stack route as audit translation residue. It rejects more
bounded Observerse fixtures as source-law evidence and pauses immediate
fresh-family selection because no new family, source variables, mature
absorbers, or falsifier are declared in this packet.

T556 selects `t557_taf11_fresh_source_law_family_preflight_gate` as the next
TAF11 burden. That next packet may run only as a fresh-family preflight; it may
not reuse the absorbed Observerse protocol-stack route as evidence.
