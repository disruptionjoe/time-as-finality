- an absorber is tested without its normal state/comparison rights;
- a mature absorber fails its ordinary control case;
- any bounded fixture is absorbed by ordinary sheaf gluing, resource transport,
  consensus/state-machine completion, or record-provenance completion;

- target labels, Lorentzian structure, cross-repo truth, Observerse replay,
  APRD replay, TAF8 execution, direct TAF4 movement, S1 movement, or public
  posture enters the success criterion;

  publication, or cross-repo truth are moved.
