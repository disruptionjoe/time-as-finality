# Landscape Reassessment

**Family:** explore
**Mode:** both
**Status:** placeholder (Phase 2). Behavior designed in Phase 3.

## Purpose
Re-survey the topology of the whole research landscape given current knowledge.
Distinct from synthesis: asks *how has the landscape itself changed?* — which
regions are promoted, which collapsed, which adjacencies opened, and whether the
primary line is still on the most promising ridge.

## Expected inputs
The full `registries/line-registry.md`; `registries/information-portfolio.md`;
`ROADMAP.md`, `NORTH-STAR.md`, `audits/`.

## Expected outputs
A reassessment note (promoted/collapsed regions, new adjacencies, primary-ridge
check); proposed lifecycle changes for `lifecycle-review`; run record; verdict.

## Related registries
`line-registry` (read), `information-portfolio` (read).

## Open questions
Cadence. How does reassessment authority interact with `line-review` and
`lifecycle-review`?
