The winner is not treated as truth. It is the current lead interpretation for
routing next work.

## Lead Interpretation

The current lead is:

> TaF as admissibility and reconstruction audit for observer-bounded records.

Reasons:

- It explains the recurring motifs: witness, provenance, admissibility,
  settlement, observer access, future-operation availability, and demotion.
- It generates immediate tasks without requiring physics-level proof first.
- It gives a clean falsification posture: if a witness, certificate, or
  admissibility rule cannot be independently checked, the local claim weakens.
- It connects naturally to Architecture of Legitimacy without pretending that
  institutional legitimacy and physical invariance are the same object.
- It keeps the aggressive North Star alive as a target-level vision rather than
  a belief already licensed by current artifacts.

## Minority Research Programs

Physics-adjacent reconstruction remains the highest-upside minority program.
It should regain lead status only if a typed capability-projection bridge
survives native absorber tests and no-go discipline.

Category theory of forgetting remains a strong formal spine. It should gain
status if witness obligations or LossKernel-style objects become invariant
rather than notational.

Information theory remains a useful measurement program. It should gain status
if future-operation availability can be quantified in a way that predicts
failures not already captured by generic information loss.

Observer epistemology remains useful but too broad unless observers are defined
by capability and access structure.

Complexity science remains useful for emergence framing but currently has weaker
claim-control than the audit and formal-forgetting interpretations.

## Lead Interpretation Loss Conditions

The lead should lose status if:

- the typed capability-projection test produces a physics-facing invariant that
  cannot be reduced to audit/admissibility language;
- a categorical witness-obligation theorem becomes the simpler explanation;
- quantitative information measures explain the live artifacts with fewer
  primitives;
- legitimacy/audit language starts importing authority assumptions that cannot
  be modeled inside TaF.

## Verdict

For the next research cycle, route work through admissibility, reconstruction,
and witness obligations. Preserve physics-adjacent reconstruction as the high
upside minority program, not as the current controlling interpretation.
