## Plain-English finding

The early repo spine is strongest where it converts intuitive finality language
into typed finite objects: record graphs, observer-local access, projection
obstruction, typed transport, and finite descent. It is weakest wherever a
finite witness might be overread as physics, continuum geometry, consciousness,
or a global time theorem. Most cards below therefore preserve useful formal
machinery while naming the absorber or boundary that blocks overclaim.
